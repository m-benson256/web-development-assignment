About Us

Ntare School is a residential and prestigious all-boys' secondary school located in Mbarara, Mbarara District, south-western Uganda. It was founded in 1956 by a Scottish educator named William Crichton.



We are a student-based learning institution with well-seasoned teaching and non-teaching staff that support the development of the School. The leadership of this great institution hails from the Board of Governors and the Headmaster.

Why Choose Ntare School?

The long-term reputation for academic excellence is only the tip of the iceberg when it comes to Ntare School.

On numerous occasions, Ntare School has emerged African, East African, National, and Regional champions in stiff competitions including Debate, Scouting, Rugby, Hockey and many others.

Our school is renowned for producing the finest all-round professionals in Medicine, Law, Engineering and beyond — and has had the rare privilege of grooming two sitting Presidents.
