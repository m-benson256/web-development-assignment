Discipline & Character Formation

Discipline at Ntare School is a cornerstone — cultivating self-respect, responsibility, and moral integrity. Our balanced code of conduct blends firmness with mentorship to produce well-rounded leaders.

🏅 Core Values

  • Integrity – Honesty in academics & conduct
  • Respect – For staff, peers, and property
  • Responsibility – Ownership of actions & duties
  • Punctuality – Time management excellence

📜 Code of Conduct

  • Uniform & Grooming – High standards always
  • Anti-Bullying – Zero tolerance, safe environment
  • Academic Integrity – No cheating, honor system
  • Attendance – Full participation expected

🧑‍🏫 Mentorship & Support

  • Guidance Counseling – Professional support staff
  • Prefect System – Student leadership & democracy
  • Restorative Practices – Mediation & growth
  • Parent Collaboration – Workshops & engagement

🏆 Awards & Recognition

Morning parade, structured study, co-curricular engagement, evening prep — building discipline as a lifelong habit.

Notable alumni credit Ntare’s discipline for success in law, medicine, military, enterprise, and public service.
